South Asia Youth Network

The South Asia Youth Network also called as SAYN, a sub-regional youth network of the Asia Pacific Youth Network (APYN). It provides a platform for youth volunteers of the National Societies of south Asia to interact, meet and remain in communication for mutual understanding, service to humanity, capacity building, sharing best practices, fostering youth action, promote youth in decision making process by considering doing more, doing better, reaching further. This regional network will support peer-to-peer approach, involve youth in service delivery and strengthen principles & values amongst the National Societies and its youth volunteers. The Network is based around the support provided by the South Asian National Societies, International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies and the Asia Pacific Youth Network, while in accordance with the Solferino Youth Declaration 2009, Ajloun Commitment 2010 and the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Strategy 2020.
